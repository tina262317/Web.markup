And most importantly, education!

  • For children with special needs

    • The Auris Prof. van Gils School, located at the heart of the neighbourhood offers splendid education for your child and takes good care of vulnerable children. The school is cozy, clean and fun and each student gets the outmost attention that they deserve. They provide education to pupils with a severe language development disorder (TOS) and to hearing-impaired pupils (SH).

    The school has a wonderful and engaging playground and is surrounded by beautiful nature where your child can freely play in!

    Check out their website for more information

  • Elementary school

    • Worrying about your child’s elementary school? By living in Oosterduin, you don’t need to! In the very near of the neighbourhood, you will find the most beautiful elementary school.

    The school has provided students with high-quality education since 1931. Students at the school are prioritized not only for their academic accomplishments but also for their safety and comfort. They want to prepare your children for life in a global society and help them develop into successful, ethical, and creative individuals and reach their full potential.

    Enroll your child in Vondelschool and open doors to a world of opportunities.

    Check out their website to learn more about what they do!
